What to expect
Use the Swiss Travel Pass and get access to unlimited rides on trains, boats, buses, and premium panorama trains across the country for 3, 4, 8, or 15 consecutive or non-consecutive days! Choose between printing out your pass yourself on an A4 paper or receive your paper pass via courier with free delivery. Once you're in Switzerland, just present your pass at the station to begin visits to Switzerland's most popular destinations like the lively city of Zurich or the Matterhorn Mountain in Zermatt, which inspired the now-iconic logo of Toberlone. Swiss Travel Passes also come with a complimentary Swiss Family Card for groups with children aged 6-15 plus free access to 500+ museums and galleries and discounts on other means of transportation!
Things to Note
Confirmation
  • You will receive confirmation of your booking instantly via email. In the event that you do not receive an email from us, please check your Spam folder or notify us via email
Booking Guidelines
  • Important: Make sure to input the correct information. Once confirmed, no changes to the booking (e.g. activation date, ticket type, & traveler information) can be made
  • If you’ve resided in country A for over 6 months but are a passport holder of country B, either fill in A as your country of residence at checkout and provide your passport and proof of residence while on the train or fill in B as your country of residence at checkout and present your passport while on the train
  • This pass is for non-Liechtenstein/Switzerland residents only. You must currently be staying in a different country for 6+ months and provide proof of residence (visa/residence card) and proof of citizenship (passport)
Pass Eligibility
  • Children aged 0-5 can travel free of charge provided they will not occupy separate seats
  • Please indicate the total number of adults, youths, and children aged 6-15 when booking
  • Costs of children's seat reservations are additional to the Swiss Family Card
  • Children aged 6-15 with at least 1 parent (including step/foster parent)/legal guardian are free of charge with the complimentary Swiss Family Card
Additional Information
  • Important: Swiss Travel Passes have character limits ⁠- names will be truncated if they are too long
  • Please note: Mountain Excursion discounts will be available until 31 Dec 2020 only. Discounts will be renewed and announced every year
  • Routes with Free Travel: No need to purchase a separate ticket in advance - just show your pass and passport/proof of residence when the conductor checks your ticket on the train
  • Routes with Discounted Travels: Purchase tickets individually (online/ticket booths) and present your pass for the discount
Insider Tips:
  • If you’re staying for a month in Switzerland, you can book the Swiss Half Fare Card for your travel needs instead!

Activity Q&A
Q: Can the booking be cancelled?
A: Cancellation Policy For Consecutive Pass: Full refunds are only possible one day prior to the date selected during booking. Thereafter, no refunds will be issued if the pass is already in use.
Cancellation Policy Flexible Pass: Full refunds are only possible one day prior to the first travel date selected upon manual activation. Thereafter, a 15% penalty applies to unused, unactivated passes. No refunds will be issued if travel days are already activated and used. Make sure all activated days are deactivated before proceeding with the refund.
Special Policy for Promotional Passes: Request an 85% refund at least one day prior to the date selected during booking. Passes cannot be refunded after this date.
